Presentation

Hi friendly visitor and welcome to my web site!
If you want to know my story with the web, here is the shortest version.

At first, I was interested in the marketing and the music industry and of course I was coding on my free time. For those who are curious, I was training myself with a French web site called "Le Site du Zero" (which is now called OpenClassrooms).
And every time I was changing my job (ok, every time is just three times), I was hoping that perhaps I would have coding work to do. And of course, it has never happened.

In March 2016, I have decided to change my life and to start coding for a living. I have started working with TreeHouse (one of the best resources of the web) and in July 2016, I have quit my job, I have started a techdegree of front end web development with Treehouse and a bachelor degree with the IESA (Institut d'Etudes Sup√©rieures des Arts) in partnership with OpenClassrooms.

So, to be honest with you, friendly reader, I am like this annoying but really cool and funny guy who is always trying to learn something new. This could be a new programming language, a new time management technique or figure out how physical sciences work.
Some people call me a nerd but I strongly prefer the term lifelong learner.

Oh, and many thanks for passing by. Do not hesitate to send me an e-mail if you want, I do look forward to hearing from you
