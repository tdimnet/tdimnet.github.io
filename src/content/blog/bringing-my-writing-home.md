For years my writing lived on Medium. It was convenient, and it found readers —
but it was never really mine. The layout, the paywall, the recommendations: none
of them were choices I made. So I am bringing everything home.

## A space that is mine

From now on, every article lives here, on my own site, with a few pieces
cross-posted to the NX Academy blog. No paywall, no distractions — just the
writing, in the same pixel-art world as the rest of my work.

> Owning your words means owning the way they are read.

## What to expect

- Pieces on software, teaching, and the occasional detour into economics.
- English and French, depending on who I am writing for.
- An open invitation to discuss: every post links back to GitHub.

This is a starting point, not a finished archive. Older favourites will migrate
over time — and new writing will land here first.
